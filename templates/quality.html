Quality: About This Data Set

This data set consists of Google activity recognition data and location data taken from a Samsung Android phone using the AWARE Framework. Data was collected from 2/17/2018 to 3/4/2018.

Is the data complete?

The data is, unfortunately not complete. First, looking at a plot of the data values over time reveals a period of missed data collection

Sometime during the 19th of February, the SQL database lost the account that the AWARE framework was using to access the database and store collected data. It took until the 24th of Febrauary for me to realize the issue and correct it, so all of the data between those dates was lost. Despite this lost data, there is data while I was on vacation in Chicago and data while at work back in Pittsburgh, so there is data available to answer the question of there being differences in activity, as recognized by the phione between vacation and work.

Is the data coherent?

To investigate data coherence, I first turned to making sure the locations that were collected were collect. Below is a geo plot of all the collected coordinates:

From a visual insepction of all the location coordinates, they do all appear in either Chicago and Pittsburgh, which is consistent with what was expected from data collection. I then looked at the distribution of activities predicted by the Google activity prediction plug in to see if the activities predicted make sense:

Once again the data makes sense, as still has the greatest count and the other much less, since most of the time I am not moving with my phone (I work at a desk for around 8 hours a day, and sleep between 6-8 hours a night). There are a fair number of unknown instances where the Google plug in could not identify what activity was taking place. This data represents some bad data in this case, as it cannot be reliably mapped to any activity category, but it also does not appear to detract from the expected distribution of activities.

Is the data correct?

Generally speaking the data is correct, however, it should be noted that conclusions drawn from it may not be generalizable, as the data only has one subject - me. When cleaning the data, there were no corrupted or incorrect data entries that had to be corrected. Data cleaning only focused on validating coherence and completeness of the data. To answer the questions of determining the differences in activity between time and location, I binned the locations and times. For the locations, there were only two main locations of interest - home and not home, and so the locations were binned into either Pittsburgh or Chicago. The times were binned into 3 different categories for two reasons. First, the times of morning, afternoon, and night, generally have qualitative differences in the activities that I do, and so should be better reflected in the activity recognition data. And, second, data is typically only uploaded by the AWARE framework when I came into contact with a WiFi point, which may only happen during certain periods of the day. So, to smooth these timestamps, I choose a broader, three-label definition of time.
