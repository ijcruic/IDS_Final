Quality: About This Data Set

This data set consists of the roll call vote faction detection results for all proposed votes on the floor of the Ukrainian Parliament. Our Research Groups has also enriched this data by adding in attributes about the Ukrainian Parliamentarians (i.e. age, method of election, profession, etc.)

Is the data complete?

This data set is not yet complete. We are in the process of the collecting archival votes from previous convocations of the parliament. We are also in the process of researching the attributes of the parliamentarians of both the current and prior convocations. However, the voting records are complete and so the factions discovered by the roll call votes alone can be considered reliable and reflective of the actual voting of the parliament.

Is the data coherent?

There have been no identified coherence issues with the roll call data. The distributions of vote repsonses matches what our subject matter experts have told us to expect (i.e. mostly 'yay' votes, with a good number of 'abstain' votes and very few 'nay' votes).

Is the data correct?

The data was taken directly from the digital records of the Ukrainian Palriament and so are as correct as we can make them.
