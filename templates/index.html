Analysis of Ukrainian Parliamentarian Voting Behaviour

Can we find factions of parliamentarians in the Ukrainian Parliament by their voting behavior? And do attributes of individuals parliamentarians contribute to these factions?

The goal of this website is to analyze factions within the body politic of the Ukrainian Parliament. In this context, a faction is a informal group of individuals with a common political interest that are acting in general concert toward that objective. With this website you can first explore different methods of comparing parliamentarians by their voting behavior and visually observe how the factions look and how they change over time and based on the method. Next, you can then go through the analysis of relating parliamentarian attributes to faction dynamics. Finally, in the about page you can learn about the data and cleaning procedures used for these analyses.
